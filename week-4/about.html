About the Portal

Built to make student data easy to act on

EduAnalytics started as a simple idea: teachers shouldn't need three different spreadsheets to answer one question about a student. It brings grades, attendance, and reports together into one clear, always up-to-date view.

What the Portal Offers

Centralized Student Records

Every student's grades, attendance, and subject history live in one organized profile, so nothing gets lost between spreadsheets or emails.

Real-Time Performance Tracking

Dashboards update as new marks and attendance are entered, giving teachers and admins a current picture instead of a stale end-of-term report.

Ready-to-Share Reports

Generate clean, per-student or per-class reports in a few clicks — useful for parent meetings, staff reviews, or school records.
